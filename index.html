Common questions

Everything you need to know before working with us.

We primarily serve high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and institutional investors looking to allocate to digital assets with professional-grade oversight. We also advise Web3 founders and protocol teams on strategy and tokenomics.

Our advisory services don't require a minimum portfolio size, but our most comprehensive strategy engagements are best suited to clients with $250,000 or more in investable capital. Reach out to discuss the right service tier for your situation.

Most crypto advisors come from either finance or crypto — rarely both. Our team bridges that gap. We apply rigorous quantitative research frameworks from traditional finance while maintaining native fluency in blockchain technology and DeFi markets.

We are primarily an advisory and research firm. We provide strategies, analysis, and recommendations — clients retain control of their own assets. We do not take custody of funds.
